## Theorem

Let $$x_1, x_2, x_3, ... , x_n \in \mathbb{R}^{+}$$. Then, $$ GM (geometric mean) \leq HM (Harmonic mean) \leq AM (arithmetic mean), in mathematical notation:

$$ \frac{n}{\frac{1}{x_1} + \frac{1}{x_2} + ... + \frac{1}{x_n}} \leq \sqrt[n]{x_1x_2...x_n} \leq \frac{x_1+x_2+...+x_n}{n}$$
